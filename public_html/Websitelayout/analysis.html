Freedom or Force?

The nomads in Zhao’s world are not rebels by choice—they are casualties of capitalism. Fern’s wandering life symbolizes the illusion of freedom within economic despair. The so-called American dream has turned into constant labor and displacement.

New Meaning of Home

Fern’s van becomes both her shelter and symbol of independence. By rejecting stability, she defines home as movement itself. The minimalism of her lifestyle challenges consumer culture and traditional success narratives.
